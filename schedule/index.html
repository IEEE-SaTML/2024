Schedule 2024

The schedule is still subject to change. Each paper accepted to the conference will be presented in the form of:

  • A 15 min talk followed by a 5 min Q&A session
  • A poster at the poster session

The poster sessions are an opportunity for attendees to continue conversations with the paper authors.
